ABOUT US

WHY THINKING?

Thinking was created to let you adventure and be limitless, and with all of our only-subscriber pings, you can relax and not get bugged by all those pings from other servers. And with our advanced moderation team that knows when to ban, kick, mute, or delete messages, you can relax and be stress-free, guaranteed.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to create a Discord community with no pings and flexible moderation. And we plan to keep Thinking a safe, wonderful, welcoming, and caring environment.
